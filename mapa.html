O Grupo Energisa foi fundado em 1905 em Cataguases (MG) como Companhia Força e Luz Cataguazes-Leopoldina. É um dos grupos mais antigos do setor elétrico brasileiro. The Energisa Group was founded in 1905 in Cataguases (MG) as Company Force and Luz Cataguazes-Leopoldina.It is one of the oldest groups in the Brazilian electricity sector.It has grown by acquisitions and today operates in distribution, transmission and generation in 11 states, focusing on solutions and diversification (voltz, natural gas, solar).
